!2 Five Core Findings Everyone Must Know

These five facts are the '''hard constraints''' against which all cross-channel analyses must be built.

!3 1. The cross-channel link is dimensional, not factual
tbl_email.TrackingId <-> sharepoint_bronze.pages.UBSGICTrackingID — '''never''' via engagement tables (pageviews, tbl_analytics_link) directly. TrackingId is a dimension, not a fact key. (Q27)

!3 2. Only ~4% of pages have a UBSGICTrackingID
1,949 out of 48,419 pages. This means ~96% of SharePoint interactions are '''not''' attributable to a pack. The dashboard must make this explicit. (Q22)

!3 3. TrackingId adoption has only been ramping up since 2024/25
Only 986/73,930 mailings (1.3%) have a TrackingId. Default dashboard time window: from 2025 onward. (Q24)

!3 4. Refresh cadence: 2x/day iMEP, 1x/day SharePoint
 * iMEP Bronze MERGE: 00:00 + 12:00 UTC
 * SharePoint Bronze: 02:00 UTC (pages MERGE, pageviews Append-WRITE)
 * iMEP Gold CTAS: 00:23 + 12:25 UTC — '''no dashboard queries during these windows'''  (Q28)

!3 5. Email skips Silver entirely
imep_silver exists only for events. For email engagement there is '''no silver layer''' — imep_gold.final is the direct consumption endpoint (denormalized, HR-enriched). (Q26)

!3 6. Gold is classified into 4 tiers

Both gold schemas follow the same strict hierarchy (Q29):

 * '''Tier 0''' — Atomic Fact (largest tables): iMEP final 520M, SP pbi_db_interactions_metrics 84M
 * '''Tier 1''' — Pre-aggregated Timespans (Rolling Windows, Date aggregates)
 * '''Tier 2''' — Per-Mailing / Per-Page Summaries (UniqueOpens/Clicks, Engagement)
 * '''Tier 3''' — Platform & Reference Dimensions (tbl_pbi_platform_mailings, employeecontact, Calendar etc.)

'''Mental Model''': iMEP Gold = message-centric per-recipient; SharePoint Gold = page-centric analytics. TrackingId is the only conceptual bridge, and it lives at the dimension level (Tier 3).

!3 7. Storage architecture: Gold co-located, zero partitioning

All 114 Delta tables are External, across '''3 ADLS accounts''' (iMEP-Bronze, SP-Bronze, '''shared Gold'''). The shared-gold design enables cross-channel joins inside Fabric/Spark without cross-account auth. '''⚠️ No partitioning on any table''' — biggest structural performance gap. Queries on final or interactions_metrics without a time filter = full-scan risk. (Q30)
